Daniel's Sliced Pickles

Description

This is a artisan dish that is sure to wow even your finest dinner guests!

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Toaster
  • Electric Outlet

    • Steps:

      1. Open bag of bread
      2. Plug in toaster and place bread in available toaster slots
      3. Push down the lever on the toaster
      4. Wait for toast to appear magically
      5. Remove toast from toaster
      6. Enjoy your toast!
